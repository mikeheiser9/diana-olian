Diana Lawrence Mashia is the founder of Olian Solutions LLC which provides consulting services in the areas of finance, research, and operations for venture capital firms and startups. For the past ten years, she has worked with venture capital firms such as Canaan Partners. Prior to her work in venture capital, Diana served as an equity research analyst at HSBC Securities covering the consumer staples and global chemicals sectors. Beyond her work in the private sector, she has previously served as Research Director for Alliance for the Arts, a non-profit arts advocacy organization for New York City arts institutions.

She is passionate about entrepreneurship, innovation, responsible and sustainable investment, and the global movement toward greater corporate responsibility and transparency. She currently serves as a member of the Board of Trustees for Earthplace, nature discovery and environmental learning center in Westport that includes a 62-acre nature center, preschool, museum, wildlife exhibits and refuge, and water quality testing lab.
